How it works

From raw footage to ready-to-post

Five steps, a few minutes, zero editing skills required.

1

Drop your video

Paste a link (YouTube, Vimeo, Twitch, Facebook, and more) or upload a file — podcasts, interviews, vlogs, gameplay, anything long-form.

2

Tell the AI what you want

Optionally describe the clips you want (e.g. "funny moments", "business tips") and pick clip length, number of clips, caption style, audio enhance and B-roll.

3

AI does the editing

Snipoclip transcribes the video, finds the best moments, reframes them to 9:16, adds animated captions and scores each clip for virality — usually in a few minutes.

4

Review & fine-tune

Browse your clips sorted by score. Open the caption editor to tweak font, size, colour or position until it’s perfect.

5

Download or post

Grab a single clip, select several and download them all at once, or send them to your channels. Done.
