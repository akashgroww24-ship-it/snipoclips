Questions

Good to know

Everything people ask before they start.

What kinds of videos can I clip?
Podcasts, interviews, webinars, vlogs, talks and gameplay — any long-form video. Paste a link or upload a file.
Which platforms can I import from?
YouTube, Vimeo, Twitch, Facebook, LinkedIn, X and more, plus direct file upload.
How long does it take?
Most videos process in a few minutes depending on length. Longer videos take a bit more.
Which languages are supported?
Captions support 20+ languages including English and Hindi, with automatic language detection and proper fonts.
Is there a free plan?
Yes — the Free plan gives you 2 clips to try with captions and reframing, no card required.
Are free clips watermarked?
Yes, free-tier clips carry a small Snipoclip watermark. Paid plans remove it.
Can I edit the captions?
Absolutely — open any clip in the built-in editor and change the caption font, size, colour and position.
How long are my clips kept?
Clips are stored for 30 days, then auto-deleted to keep things tidy. Download anything you want to keep.
Do you offer refunds?
New paid subscribers can request a full refund within 7 days of their first payment.
Is my data secure?
Yes — your API keys stay on the server, never the browser, and accounts are protected with row-level security, rate limits and HTTPS.
